E-Temp Mail FAQs


1. Is there any software or mobile app necessary to use this service?

A. No, you just need a web browser to check your emails.

 

2. Do I have to pay any cost for this service?

A. No, There is no cost to use this service. You can enjoy the service absolutely for free.

 

3. How long can I use a temporary email address?

A. You can use a temporary email address as long as we support any domain. But the emails you received are deleted every day.

 

4. Can I delete the received emails on my own?

A. Yes, there is a delete button on every email, click on it to delete the email.

 

5. Can I save my emails?

A. Yes, you can download your emails. There is a download button on every email, click on it to download the email for later use.

 

6. Can I create multiple temporary email IDs?

A. For the time being there is no limit, you can create as many email IDs as you need.

 

7. Sometimes my email takes a long time to arrive. What's the issue?

A. If you are facing a delay to get the emails, it may be due to the large number of emails we receive, or sometimes it is caused due to the network problem on the sender side. However, we apologize for the inconvenience.

Our team is dedicatedly working to improve our service. 


8. When my email got expired?

A. Your free disposable email address never expires until we support a domain. But, for users convenience we provide 10 minute mail and 5 minutes mail services.
